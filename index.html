Meeting intelligence

Transcribe, summarize, and chat with your meetings using local AI. No data leaves your device unless you choose.

Download for macOS View on GitHub

Requires macOS 26 (Tahoe) · Apple Silicon recommended

Dark Light

Everything happens on your device.

No servers, no subscriptions, no data collection. Just your meetings, your Mac, your AI.

Real-Time Transcription

Parakeet, WhisperKit, or Apple Speech — all running on your Mac. No audio ever leaves your device.

Private by Default

All data stored locally via SwiftData. API keys in macOS Keychain. Zero telemetry. Zero analytics.

AI Summaries

Automatic titles, multi-section summaries, key decisions, and action items — powered by local MLX or cloud LLMs.

Chat With Meetings

Ask questions about any meeting. "What did we decide about the API?" — instant answers from your transcripts.

Action Items

Auto-extracted with priority levels and deadlines. Route to Notion, ClickUp, Apple Reminders, or n8n webhooks.

Voice Assistant

Push-to-talk with an animated orb UI. Ask about your action items, upcoming meetings, or past discussions — fully local.

Three steps. Zero config.

1

Download & Launch

Clone the repo and run ./run.sh. Or download the DMG from GitHub Releases. Works out of the box — no API keys needed.

2

Record a Meeting

Click record on any calendar event — or start a quick note. A floating transcript shows words as they're captured.

3

Get Intelligence

LidIA refines the transcript, generates a summary, and extracts action items — all automatically when you stop recording.
